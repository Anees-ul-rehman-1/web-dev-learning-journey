Q 01: When is Uber Eats Pakistan launching in Pakistan?

Uber Eats Pakistan is launching in 2026. Initial cities are Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad and Rawalpindi, followed by Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta and other major cities. Pre-registered users get early access and exclusive founder discounts.

Q 02: What's the commission rate for restaurants?

Restaurants who pre-registered lock in 0% commission for the first 90 days. After that our rates are meaningfully lower than Foodpanda's typical 15—25%. There are no signup fees and no monthly subscription — you only pay when orders come in.

Q 03: Can home chefs and tiffen services list on Uber Eats Pakistan?

Yes. We have a dedicated Homemade & Tiffin category specifically for home-based cooks, ghar ka khana sellers and small tiffin services. Many cities already have established home chef communities — Karachi's DHA and Clifton, Lahore's Gulberg and DHA, Islamabad's F-sectors — and we want to help grow.

Q 04: How is the customer launch offer different from Foodpanda's vouchers?

Pre-registered customers get 50% off their first 5 order plus free delivery for 30 days — locked in the moment we launch. This is not a one-time voucher; it's a founder benefit that stacks across multiple orders.

Q 05: Will Uber Eats work during internet shutdowns?

Pakistan has experienced multiple internet shutdowns that have cut food delivery orders by 30 — 50% on affected days. We're building SMS-based order confirmation and tracking so restaurants don't lose revenue and customers can still get fed when the internet is patchy.

Q 06: Which neighbourhood will be covered first?

At launch, we'll cover the highest-density areas in each city. In Karachi: DHA, Clifton, PECHS, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Bahadurabad, Bahria Town. In lahore: DHA, Gulberg, Model Town, Johar Town, Satellite Town. Coverage expands quickly based on pre-registration densiy — so the more people sign up in you area, the sooner we arrrive.

Q 07: Is Uber Eats Pakistan halal certified?

Every listed restaurant must confirm halal compilance during onboarding. We work only with restaurants serving halal food unless the listing is explicitly marked otherwise (rare cases like specific seafood-only spots).
